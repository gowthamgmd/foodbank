# Smart Food Bank - Brief Resume Summary

**Smart Food Bank** - Full-stack platform connecting food donors to beneficiaries; reduces waste and food insecurity through real-time coordination and AI quality assessment.
Built with React, Node.js, MongoDB, Flask; 50+ APIs with JWT auth and role-based access (Donor/Beneficiary/Admin).
Features AI food quality assessment and demand forecasting reducing manual inspection by 70%.
Deployed on Vercel/Render/MongoDB Atlas: foodbank-94q8.onrender.com.
